Ujjain, Gupta Empire (c. 400 CE)

Architecture & Cityscape:
UMC invited the suggestions from citizens of Ujjain to make ‘Ujjain as a smart city’.

Daily Life & Culture:
There are also other minor centres of Gupta sculpture, particularly in the areas of Dasapura and Mandasor, where a huge eight-faced mukhalinga (probably early 6th-century) found in the river has been reinstalled in the Pashupatinath Temple, Mandsaur.
The later Ajanta style of sculpture is somewhat heavy, but sometimes "awe-inspiring" in the large seated shrine Buddhas, but other smaller figures are often very fine, as is the ornamental carving on columns and door-frames.
