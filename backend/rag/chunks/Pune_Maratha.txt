Pune, Maratha Empire (c. 1750 CE)

Economy & Trade:

Architecture & Cityscape:
There are several Peths in usual localities of the Pune city.
Shinde Chhatri, located at Wanowrie, is a memorial dedicated to the Maratha general Mahadaji Shinde (Scindia).

Daily Life & Culture:
Pune is also the birthplace of Meher Baba, although his followers usually travel to Meherabad to visit his tomb.
Hazrat Babajan, identified by Meher Baba as one of the five perfect masters, has a shrine (Dargah) erected in her honour under a neem tree in Pune Camp.
