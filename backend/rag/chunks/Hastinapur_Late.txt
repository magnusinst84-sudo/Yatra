Hastinapur, Late Vedic Period (c. 900 BCE)

Political & Security:
Situated in the historic location of the ancient city of Hastinapur, the Pandeshwar temple is dedicated to Shiva.
This is a small Gurdwara located in the village of Saifpur, around 2.5 km (1.6 mi) from Hastinapur.

Architecture & Cityscape:
The Karna Temple is located near the Pandeshwar temple on an old ravine along the bank of the Ganges.
The Shivling inside the Karna Temple is believed to be established by Karna, one of the prominent figures in the Mahabharata.
